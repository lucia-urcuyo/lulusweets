## Ingredients

| Ingredients |  |
| :---- | :---: |
| Unsalted Butter | 68 |
| Water | 75 |
| While Milk | 75 |
| All Purpose Flour | 105 |
| Eggs | (3) |
| Salt | 6 |
| Sugar | 6.3 |
| Vanilla | 4 |

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 °F and line two baking sheets with silicon baking mats.
2. Add water, milk, butter, sugar, and salt to a medium sized saucepan.
3. Once the mixture comes to a gentle simmer, remove from heat and stir in the flour all at once with a wooden spoon or a spatula.
4. One flour is incorporated, place back over medium heat about 1 1/2 to 2 minutes stirring constantly to release extra moisture and partially cook flour, or until dough comes together into a smooth ball and a thin film forms on bottom of pan.
5. Transfer the dough to a large mixing bowl with a paddle attachment and mix on low for 2-3 minutes to cool the mixture slightly.
6. Add the eggs one a time, allowing each egg to fully incorporate before adding the next.
7. Beat until dough is smooth and forms a thick ribbon when pulled up.
8. Transfer to a piping bag fitted with a medium round tip for cream puffs or medium open star tip for eclairs and pipe them 1 1/2" apart.
9. Bake for 50 minutes, without opening the oven.
10. Once baked, pierce a couple of holes in the bottom of each choux, if they are not hollow, place back in the oven for 10-15 minutes.
11. Fill with choice of filling.
