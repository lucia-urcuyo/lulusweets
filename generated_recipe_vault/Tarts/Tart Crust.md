## Ingredients

| Ingredients | 4 |
| :---- | ----- |
| Unsalted Butter | 70 |
| All Purpose Flour | 122 |
| Almond Flour | 15 |
| Powdered Sugar | 65 |
| Almond Extract | 1 |
| Egg yolk | (1) |

## Instructions

1. Combine butter and icing sugar in a stand mixer. Whip until light and fluffy.
2. Add egg yolk and almond extract, mix until combined.
3. Add the all-purpose flour and almond flour one spoonful at a time. The dough should be crumbly.
4. Form into a small disc and pop in the fridge, let sit for at least an hour before rolling out.
5. Preheat the oven to 350 F.
6. Roll out the chilled dough onto a floured surface. Mold the dough into the tart molds. Pierce holes at the bottom and pop it into the freezer for around 15-20 minutes.
7. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until golden brown.
8. Remove from the oven and allow shells to cool completely.
