title: "Balsamic Caramel"
category: "sauces"
---

## Ingredients

| Ingredients |  |
| :---- | :---: |
| Brown Sugar | 100 |
| Balsamic Vinegar | 59 |
| Heavy Cream | 118 |
| Butter | 14 |
| Salt | 1 |

## Instructions

1. Place brown sugar and balsamic vinegar in a small saucepan. Bring to a gentle boil.
2. Let mixture simmer over medium heat for about 3 minutes, until thickened and reduced.
3. Add heavy cream.
4. Bring it back to a boil, and simmer again over medium heat for another 2-4 minutes, until thick.
5. Add butter and salt. Mix to combine, until butter is melted.
6. Remove and transfer to a bowl. Let it cool down.
