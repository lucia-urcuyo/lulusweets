title: "Cranberry Sauce Filling"
category: "sauces"
---

## Ingredients

| Ingredients |  |
| :---- | :---: |
| Cranberries | 98 |
| Granulated Sugar | 37.5 |
| Orange Juice | 62.9 |
| Orange Zest | 6 |

## Instructions

1. Place all ingredients in a small saucepan.

2. Cook over medium low heat for about 10-15 minutes, stirring every now and then.

3. If the compote is getting dry, consider adding a tablespoon of water or so.

4. Once the compote is thick, and cranberries have fallen apart, remove to a bowl. Cover and let it chill in the fridge.
