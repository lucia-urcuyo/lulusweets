---
title: "Nutella Cremeux"
category: "sauces"
---

## Ingredients

| Ingredients |  |
| :---- | :---: |
| Nutella | 50 |
| Milk Chocolate | 100 |
| Heavy Cream | 200 |
| Half and half/whole milk | 120 |
| Granulated Sugar | 35 |
| Egg Yolks | (2) |
| Salt | 5 |

## Instructions

1. Add milk chocolate and Nutella to a heat proof bowl and set aside.

2. In a medium saucepan, combine heavy cream, milk, sugar, and salt. Set over medium heat and stir constantly.

3. When it begins to steam, remove from the heat and add a third of the cream mixture into the egg yolks while stirring to temper the yolks.

4. Return to heat and continue heating the milk until it is about to boil.

5. Lower the heat and add the tempered egg yolks. Whisk constantly.

6. When the mixture thickens and forms a custard, remove from the heat and set aside.

7. Place a mesh sieve over the milk chocolate and Nutella and pass the custard through the sieve.

8. Stir the chocolate into the custard until it is fully combined.

9. Transfer to an airtight container and store in the fridge.
