## Ingredients

| Ingredients |  |
| :---- | :---: |
| Chocolate Chips | 340.00 |
| Heavy Cream | 201.00 |
| Baileys/ Espresso Rum | 28.00 |

## Instructions

1. Place chocolate into a medium sized bowl.
2. Pour heavy cream and Baileys in a medium-sized pot, and heat at a medium high heat, until it just begins to boil.
3. Pour over the chocolate, and allow the mixture to sit for 1-2 minutes.
4. Slowly stir with a rubber spatula, until the mixture is smooth. 5, Once the ingredients are fully incorporated, place plastic wrap over the top of the ganache, and set aside.
