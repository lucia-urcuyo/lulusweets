## Ingredients

| Ingredients |  |
| :---- | :---: |
| Frozen Blueberries | 400 |
| Balsamic vinegar | 25 |
| Vanilla Extract | 1 |
| Granulated Sugar | 80 |
| Salt | 3 |

## Instructions

1. Place all the ingredients in a small saucepan over medium heat, and cook, stirring often, until the mixture comes to a boil.

2. Cook, stirring constantly, for 7-9 minutes or until reduced to desired consistency.

3. Transfer to a container, cover, and place in the fridge to chill completely.
