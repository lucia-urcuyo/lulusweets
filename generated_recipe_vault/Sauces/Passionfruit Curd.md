## Ingredients

| Ingredients |  |
| :---- | :---: |
| Sugar | 84 |
| Passionfruit | 120 |
| Butter | 84 |
| Egg yolks | (4) |

## Instructions

1. Add all of the ingredients into a small saucepan.
2. Start cooking at low heat, stirring non-stop with a spatula.

3. Keep cooking for 5-10 minutes. Don’t let the mixture boil, and don’t stop stirring.
4. Once the curd is ready, pour it into a heat-proof bowl, and let it cool down. The curd should be thick, coating the back of a spoon.

5. Place it in the fridge for at least 6 hours to chill through and get thick.
