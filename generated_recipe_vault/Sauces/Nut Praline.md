## Ingredients

| Ingredients |  |
| :---- | :---: |
| Nuts | 150 |
| Sugar | 100 |

## Instructions

1. Combine nuts and sugar in a medium saucepan.
2. Place over medium heat and let the sugar begin to melt.

3. Once the sugar begins to melt, stir to coat the nuts evenly.
4. The caramelized nuts should be a deep amber color and fragrant when removed from the heat.

5. Pour onto a silicone baking sheet or parchment paper and let it cool.

6. Once it is fully cooled, break into pieces.

7. Transfer into a high-power blender and blend on medium speed.

8. Remove from the blender when it reaches desired consistency, it will first become a crumble and then turn into a paste.
