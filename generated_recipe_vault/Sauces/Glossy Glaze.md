category: "sauces"
---

## Ingredients

| Ingredients |  |
| :---- | :---: |
| Unsalted Butter | 225 |
| Powdered Sugar | 650 |
| Boiling Water | 60 |
| Vanilla Extract | 6 |

## Instructions

1. Melt the butter and add it to the powdered sugar.

2. Add half of the hot water and whisk to combine, the consistency will be thick.

3. Add hot water one tablespoon at a time, whisking after each addition until desired consistency,

4. End result should be thick and glossy.

5. Add vanilla and white food coloring.
