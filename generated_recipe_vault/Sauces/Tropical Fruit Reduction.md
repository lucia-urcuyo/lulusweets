---
title: "Tropical Fruit Reduction"
category: "sauces"
---

## Ingredients

| Ingredients |  |
| :---- | :---: |
| Frozen Pineapple | 100 |
| Frozen Mango | 250 |
| Passionfruit Pulp | 165 |
| Granulated Sugar | 65 |

## Instructions

1. Blend all the fruit in a food processor or a blender.
2. Add the blended fruits and sugar into a medium saucepan over medium heat.
3. Stir frequently to ensure nothing sticks to the bottom of the pan or burns
4. When the reduction has thickened and coats the back of your spoon, remove from heat.
5. Pass through a mesh sieve if necessary.
6. Transfer the reduction to an airtight container. Let it cool and store it in the fridge.
