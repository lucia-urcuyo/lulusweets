## Ingredients

| Ingredients |  |
| :---- | :---: |
| Pumpkin Puree | 280 |
| Cream Cheese | 450 |
| Powdered Sugar | 100 |
| Vanilla Extract | 4.2 |
| Ground Cinnamon | 1 |
| Pumpkin Pie Spice | 2 |
| Salt | 3 |

## Instructions

1. Place the pumpkin into a medium saucepan.

2. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, for 4-5 minutes until reduced slightly. Set aside to cool, and then measure out 200g of reduced pumpkin.

3. Place the reduced pumpkin along with all other ingredients in a medium bowl and whip until well incorporated.
