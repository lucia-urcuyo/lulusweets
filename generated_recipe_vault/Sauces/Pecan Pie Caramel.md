## Ingredients

| Ingredients |  |
| :---- | :---: |
| Toasted, Crushed Pecans | 81 |
| Sugar | 100 |
| Light Corn Syrup | 15 |
| Heavy Cream | 80 |
| Butter | 21 |
| Bourbon | 15 |

## Instructions

1. Place the sugar, light corn syrup, and bourbon in a saucepan over medium heat and melt the sugar while stirring continuously.
2. When the sugar has melted and it is a golden color, lower the heat to low and add the heavy cream.
3. Continue to stir the heavy cream for a few seconds until the bubbles subside and the syrup is smooth again.
4. Quickly add the butter and stir.
5. Once the butter has melted almost entirely, remove the pan from the heat.
6. Add the pecans and stir to coat.
7. Transfer the caramel to a heatproof bowl and let it cool before using.
