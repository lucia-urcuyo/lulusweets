## Ingredients

| Ingredients |  |
| :---- | :---: |
| Banana | 2 |
| Whole Milk | 240 |
| Granulated Sugar | 100 |
| Espresso Shots | 2 |
| Vanilla | 2 |
| Salt | pinch |

## Instructions

1. Blend everything
2. Pour into a saucepan. Cook on medium-low, stirring often.
3. After it steams, lower to low. Simmer for 45–60 min, stirring every few minutes, until thick and caramel-like.
