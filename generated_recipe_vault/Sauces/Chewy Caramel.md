## Ingredients

| Ingredients |  |
| :---- | :---: |
| Sugar | 260 |
| Water | 105 |
| Heavy Cream | 245 |
| Butter | 45 |
| Sea Salt | 6 |
| Vanilla Extract | 4.2 |

## Instructions

4. Combine sugar and water in a medium saucepan, place over medium heat. Let it come to a simmer and keep an eye on it.
5. When the sugar syrup reaches a light amber color add in the heavy cream and give it a quick mix.
6. Cook caramel to 235-240 F, it will become a caramel color and thicken up.
7. Add butter and mix in.
8. Add vanilla and salt. Transfer to a heatproof bowl and let it cool before using.
