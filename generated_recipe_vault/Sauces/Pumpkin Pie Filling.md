category: "sauces"
---

## Ingredients

| Ingredients |  |
| :---- | :---: |
| Mascarpone | 100 |
| Heavy Cream | 100 |
| Pumpkin Puree | 50 |
| Pumpkin Pie Spice | 2 |
| Powdered Sugar | 40 |
| Vanilla Extract | 4.2 |
| Salt | 3 |

## Instructions

1. Combine sugar and heavy cream and whisk until a whipping cream forms.

2. Add the mascarpone and whip briefly to incorporate.

3. Add the pumpkin, spices, vanilla and salt, and whisk until fully incorporated.
