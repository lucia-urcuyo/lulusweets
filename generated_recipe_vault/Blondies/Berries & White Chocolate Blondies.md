## Ingredients

| Ingredients |  |
| :---- | :---: |
| Browned Butter | 135 |
| Light Brown Sugar | 338 |
| Eggs | 3 |
| Vanilla Extract | 7 |
| Flour | 270 |
| Baking Powder | 3 |
| Salt | 4 |
| White Chocolate Chips | 150 |
| Caramelized White Chocolate Chunks | 150 |
| Berry Sauce |  |

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350F. Line a 8 x 8 baking pan with parchment paper.
2. In a medium saucepan, brown the butter over medium heat. Allow to cool slightly.
3. Add brown sugar and whisk.
4. Add the eggs and whisk.
5. Add vanilla and whisk.
6. In a separate bowl, mix the flour, baking powder, and salt together.
7. Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and whisk. Switch to a spatula when needed.
8. Fold in the white chocolate chips and caramelized white chocolate chunks.
9. Pour batter into the pan, smoothing the top with a spatula.
10. Inject berry sauce into the blondies.
11. Bake until the blondies are set an inch a way into the tin and still wobbly in the center, approximately 35 minutes.
12. Let the blondies chill in the fridge before cutting into bars.
