## Ingredients

| Ingredients |  |
| :---- | :---: |
| Butter | 255 |
| Brown Sugar | 300 |
| Eggs | 2 |
| Vanilla Extract | 12.6 |
| Flour | 300 |
| Baking Powder | 6 |
| Salt | 4.5 |
| [[Chewy Caramel]], divided | 444 |

## Instructions

1. In a medium saucepan, brown the butter over medium heat.
2. Add brown sugar and 244 g of caramel. Stir it for 1-2 minutes until the mixture is nice and glossy and all of the sugars are dissolved into the butter.
3. Set aside and allow to cool for about 15 minutes.
4. Heat oven to 350 degrees. Line a 8 x 8 baking pan with parchment paper.
5. Add vanilla and then eggs one at a time, stirring after each addition.
6. Stir in flour, baking powder, and salt. Pour batter into the pan of choice, smoothing the top with a spatula.
7. Drizzle the 200 g caramel that are left all over the top of the batter.
8. Bake until the top is shiny and slightly cracked, approximately 50 minutes. Let cool before cutting into bars.
