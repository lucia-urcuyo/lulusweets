## Ingredients

| Ingredients |  |
| :---- | :---: |
| Pastry Flour | 773 |
| Unsalted Butter | 510 |
| Water | 139 |
| Milk | 139 |
| Sugar | 23 |
| Salt | 15 |

## Instructions

1. Scale ingredients.
2. Put a part of the flour in flat surface.
3. Put slices of butter on top of the flour, add more flour.
4. Roll until butter is in thin layers.
5. Repeat until all butter is used.
6. Add remaining flour, sugar and salt.
7. Use a bench scraper to chop.
8. Transfer everything to a bowl.
9. Bring the water and milk together.
10. Pour half of the wet ingredients in the middle of the dry ingredients and then cover with a little bit of the dry ingredients that are on the sides.
11. Pour the rest of the wet ingredients in a circular motion all around the dry ingredients.
12. Mix very fast using your hands with your finger wide open while spinning the bowl. Do not overwork the dough.
13. Grind up, grabbing mixture and rubbing hands against each other. ** **
14. Portion and put in plastic wrap. Make a snowball, press, and shape like a hamburger patty.
15. Let the dough to rest overnight in the cooler.
