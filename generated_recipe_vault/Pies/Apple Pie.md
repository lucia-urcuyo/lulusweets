## Ingredients

| Ingredients |  |
| :---- | :---: |
| Red Apples | 3 |
| Green Apples | 3 |
| Granulated Sugar | 150 |
| All Purpose Flour | 31 |
| Lemon Juice | 28.7 |
| Lemon Zest | 4 |
| Ground Cinnamon | 3.96 |
| Ground Allspice | 0.51 |
| Ground Cloves | 0.55 |
| Ground Nutmeg | 0.59 |

## Instructions

1. Prepare an unbaked pie shell and set oven temperature to 400°F (204°C).
2. In a large bowl, stir the apple slices, sugar, flour, lemon zest, lemon juice, cinnamon, allspice, cloves, and nutmeg together until thoroughly combined. Set filling aside as the oven preheats. This gives the filling a chance to rest.
3. Spoon the filling into the crust and discard the leftover juices in the bottom of the bowl.
4. Assemble top crust, lightly brush with an egg wash and sprinkle with coarse sugar.
5. Place the pie onto a large baking sheet and bake for 25 minutes. Turn the temperature down to 375°F (190°C) and bake for an additional 30-35 minutes. After the first 20 minutes of bake time, place a pie crust shield on top of the pie to prevent the edges from browning too quickly.
6. Allow the pie to cool for 3 full hours at room temperature before serving. This time allows the filling to thicken up.
