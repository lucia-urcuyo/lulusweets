## Ingredients

| Ingredients |  |
| :---- | :---: |
| Lemon Juice | 14 |
| Water | 198 |
| Granulated Sugar | 128 |
| Salt | 3 |
| Cornstarch | 32 |
| Ground Nutmeg | 0.28 |
| Frozen Blueberries | 454 |

## Instructions

1. Prepare an unbaked pie shell and set oven temperature to 400°F (204°C).
2. Combine part of the sugar with cornstarch, salt and nutmeg in a bowl.
3. In a pot, combine the rest of the sugar with water and lemon juice.
4. Cook liquid mixture until it starts boiling.
5. Add the dry ingredients and whisk until thickened and clear.
6. Count to 12 and remove from heat.
7. Add the frozen blueberries and stir to combine.
8. Pour the berry mixture into the pie crust.
9. Apply Streusel if using. Using your fingers, make holes to allow the streusel to get inside the pie.
10. Bake for 45 minutes, or until the crust in gold, the filling rises and the filling is boiling.
