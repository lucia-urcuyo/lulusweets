category: "pies"
---

## Ingredients

| Ingredients |  |
| :---- | :---: |
| Bread Flour | 240 |
| Cinnamon | 1 |
| Salt | 3 |
| Brown Sugar | 84 |
| Granulated Sugar | 84 |
| Cold Butter | 180 |

## Instructions

1. Put a part of the flour in flat surface.
2. Put slices of butter on top of the flour, add more flour.
3. Roll until butter is in thin layers.
4. Repeat until all butter is used.
5. Add remaining flour, sugar and salt.
6. Use a bench scraper to chop.
7. Put away in cooler.
