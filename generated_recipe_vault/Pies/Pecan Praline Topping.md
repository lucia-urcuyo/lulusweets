---
title: "Pecan Praline Topping"
category: "pies"
---

## Ingredients

| Ingredients |  |
| :---- | :---: |
| Chopped Pecans | 130 |
| Packed Brown Sugar | 100 |
| Honey | 30 |

## Instructions

1. Using a fork, mix the pecans and brown sugar together. Drizzle in the honey and mix until combined.
2. Spoon on top of the pie and gently press it down with a spoon or fork so it sticks on the filling.
3. Return pie to the oven and bake for an additional 15 minutes.
