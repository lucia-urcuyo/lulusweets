## Ingredients
### Cake
Egg yolks 		85 (5)
Sugar			300
All Purpose Flour	210
Baking Powder	10.78
Salt			2.13
Milk			90
Vanilla Extract		4.2
Egg Whites		150 (5)

### Tres Leches
Condensed Milk	14 oz
Evaporated Milk	24 oz
Whole Milk		363
Vanilla Extract		4.2

### Maringue
Egg Whites		90
Cream of tartar 	1
Lemon			14.4
Sugar			201
Water			118.29

## Instructions
### For the Cake

2. Preheat the oven to 350 °F and butter a 9x13 casserole pan.
3. In a large bowl, sift together flour, baking powder, and salt. In a separate bowl, add together milk and vanilla extract.
4. Beat egg yolks with the sugar on high speed, until yolks are a pale yellow (2 minutes).
5. Gradually add 1/3 of the flour mixture, followed by ½ of the milk mixture. Repeat that process until all of the flour has been incorporated.
6. Beat egg whites on high speed until stiff peaks form.
7. Pour egg yolk mixture over the egg whites and fold with the [spatula](https://amzn.to/2QvwT9V) until just combined. Pour batter into prepared pan and spread to even out the surface and bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.

### For the Tres Leches

1. In a large measuring cup, combine the evaporated milk, the condensed milk, and the whole milk. When the cake is cool, pierce the surface all over with a fork. Slowly drizzle the milk mixture over the cake.

### For the Meringue

1. In a small saucepan over medium heat add the sugar and water. Mix with a spoon and cook until it reaches a temperature of 248º F.
2. Beat the egg whites with an electric mixer on medium speed until soft peaks, add the cream tartar and lemon. Reduce speed to low and pour hot syrup from the side of the bowl in a slow and steady stream. Increase speed to high and continue beating until stiff peaks and the mixture is cool and bright.
3. Top the cake with the meringue creating swirls and peaks.
