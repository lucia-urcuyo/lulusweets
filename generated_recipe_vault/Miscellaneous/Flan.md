## Ingredients
Eggs			5
Condensed Milk	28 oz
Evaporated Milk	24 oz
Vanilla		\`	6 g

### Syrup
Water		65
Sugar		260

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).Add water and sugar to a medium saucepan over medium-low heat and melt until liquefied and golden in color.
2. Carefully pour hot syrup into a 9-inch round or 8 inch square baking pan, turning the dish to evenly coat the bottom and sides. Set aside.
3. In a large bowl, beat eggs. Beat in condensed milk, evaporated milk and vanilla until smooth. Pour egg mixture into a baking dish.
4. Bake for 50 minutes. Let cool completely.
5. To serve, carefully invert on a serving plate when completely cool.
