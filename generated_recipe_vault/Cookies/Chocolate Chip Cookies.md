category: "cookies"

## Ingredients

| Ingredients | 24 |
| :---- | :---: |
| Unsalted Butter | 227 |
| Milk | 28 |
| Granulated Sugar | 150 |
| Dark Brown Sugar | 150 |
| Eggs | 2 |
| Vanilla Extract | 4 |
| All Purpose Flour | 260 |
| Baking Soda | 6 |
| Salt | 6 |
| Semi-Sweet Chocolate Chips | 142 |
| Milk Chocolate Chips | 142 |

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
2. Brown the butter.
3. Add milk to butter and allow it to cool down.
4. Add white granulated sugar and dark brown sugar to the butter mixture and whisk until homogeneous.
5. Add the eggs one at a time until incorporated and the mixture is smooth.
6. Add vanilla, whisk.
7. Mix the flour, baking soda, and salt in a separate bowl, and whisk.
8. Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and whisk. Switch to spatula if needed.
9. Fold in the chocolate chips.
10. Portion out the batter into 18 balls and let them rest in the fridge overnight.
11. Bake for 18-22 minutes. Allow to cool for several minutes before removing from the pan.
