title: "Ginger Molasses Chewy Cookies"
category: "cookies"
---

## Ingredients

| Ingredients | 18 |
| :---- | :---: |
| Unsalted Butter, softened | 140 |
| Brown Sugar | 110 |
| Granulated Sugar | 100 |
| Egg | 1 |
| Fancy Molasses | 80 |
| Vanilla Extract | 0.5 tsp |
| All Purpose Flour | 284 |
| Baking Soda | 1.25 tsp |
| Salt | 0.5 tsp |
| Ground Ginger | 2 tsp |
| Ground Cinnamon | 1 tsp |
| Ground Clove | 0.125 tsp |
| Crystallized Ginger, chopped | 70 |
| Granulated Sugar, for rolling | 65 |

## Instructions

1. Cream butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar until smooth and fluffy.
2. Mix in egg, molasses, and vanilla.
3. Whisk flour, baking soda, salt, ginger, cinnamon, and clove in a separate bowl.
4. Mix dry ingredients into the butter mixture just until combined.
5. Fold in crystallized ginger.
6. Cover and chill dough for 2-8 hours.
7. Preheat oven to 350°F and line baking sheets with parchment.
8. Scoop 18 dough balls and roll each one in granulated sugar.
9. Bake 9-12 minutes, until puffed, cracked, and evenly browned.
10. Cool on the baking sheet for 2 minutes, then transfer to a rack.

## Notes

- Chill 2 hours for more spread and dramatic cracks.
- Chill 8 hours for thicker cookies.
