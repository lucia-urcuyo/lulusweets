## Ingredients

| Ingredients | 4, 7 in (g) | 4, 6 in (g) | 12-14 CC |
| :---- | :---: | :---: | :---: |
| Egg Whites | 164.4 | 120 | 76.1 |
| Granulated Sugar | 274 | 200 | 126.8 |
| Unsalted Butter | 387.7 | 283 | 179.4 |
| Vanilla Extract | 5.8 | 4.2 | 2.7 |

## Instructions

1. Remove butter from the fridge about 30 minutes before you start to make this. It shouldn’t be hard cold, and it shouldn’t be so soft that is almost melting.

2. Put egg whites and sugar in a heatproof bowl.

3. Place bowl on top of a small pot with simmering water.

4. Whisk whites and sugar until they temp 140 Fahrenheit, until the sugar granules are melted and the egg white is in a safe temperature to be consumed.

5. Transfer the syrup to a stand mixer bowl with a whisk attachment. Whip for about 2 minutes at medium speed, until the mixture looks white, and thicker. Raise the speed to high and continue to whip until fluffy and doubled in size. Meringue should have stiff peaks. It can take anywhere from 5-10.

6. Start adding the butter one tablespoon at a time with mixer running on medium-high, waiting for each butter piece to be incorporated before adding the next one.

7. Add vanilla extract.
