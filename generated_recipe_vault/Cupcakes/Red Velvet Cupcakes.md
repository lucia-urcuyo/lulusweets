## Ingredients

| Ingredients | 1 dozen (g) |
| :---- | :---: |
| All Purpose Flour | 163 |
| Granulated Sugar | 207 |
| Baking Soda | 5 |
| Baking Powder | 1.5 |
| Cocoa Powder | 4 |
| Salt | 2 |
| Buttermilk | 120 |
| Vegetable Oil | 56 |
| Vanilla Extract | 4 |
| Eggs | 57 |
| White Vinegar | 5 |
| Hot Water | 120 |

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350°F (176°C) and prepare cupcake pan with liners.
2. Add the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, cocoa and salt to a large mixer bowl and combine. Set aside.
3. Add the buttermilk, vegetable oil, vanilla extract, eggs, vinegar and red food coloring to a medium sized bowl and combine.
4. Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and mix until well combined.
5. Slowly add the hot water to the batter and mix on low speed until well combined. Scrape down the sides of the bowl as needed to make sure everything is well combined.
6. Fill the cupcake liners about half way and bake for 16-18 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out with a few moist crumbs.
