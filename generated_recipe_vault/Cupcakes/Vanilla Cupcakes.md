## Ingredients

| Ingredients | 1 dozen (g) |
| :---- | :---: |
| Unsalted Butter | 84 |
| Sugar | 155 |
| Sour Cream | 86 |
| Vanilla Extract | 8 |
| Egg Whites | 90 |
| All Purpose Flour | 163 |
| Baking Powder | 8 |
| Salt | 2 |
| Milk | 93 |
| Water | 30 |

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees and prepare a cupcake pan with cupcake liners.
2. Whisk together flour, sugar, baking soda and baking powder in a large mixing bowl.
3. Add butter, egg, egg yolk, vanilla, sour cream and milk and mix on medium speed just until smooth. Do not over mix.
4. Fill cupcake liners a little more than halfway.
5. Bake 18-20 minutes.
6. Allow to cool for 1-2 minutes, then remove to cooling rack to finish cooling.
