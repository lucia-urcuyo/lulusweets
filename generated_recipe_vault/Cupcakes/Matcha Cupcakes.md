## Ingredients

| Ingredients | 1 dozen (g) |
| :---- | :---: |
| Cake Flour | 135 |
| Granulated Sugar | 150 |
| Baking Powder | 6 |
| Baking Soda | 3 |
| Salt | 2 |
| Whole Milk | 123 |
| Vegetable Oil | 112 |
| Vanilla Extract | 4 |
| Eggs | 114 |
| Matcha | 9 |

## Instructions

1. Begin by preheating oven to 350°F (175°C), and place cupcake liners in baking pans.
2. Sift the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, salt, and matcha into a large bowl.
3. Pour in the milk and oil, and gently stir until just combined. The batter will be thick, but that's how it should be!
4. Add in the vanilla and eggs and mix until the batter comes together.
5. Fill the cupcake liners about 2/3 way full. Bake for 15-17 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out clean.
6. Allow the cupcakes to cool completely.
