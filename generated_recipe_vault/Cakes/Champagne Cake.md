## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit. Grease and line cake pans.
2. Combine flour, sugar, baking powder and salt in a mixing bowl. 3\. Slowly beat in chunks of butter. Mix on low until mixture becomes crumbly. 4\. Mix in egg whites.
3. Mix in the champagne, then the buttermilk.
4. Add in oil, and vanilla, then mix at a medium speed until all ingredients are fully incorporated.
5. 7.Divide batter between the four prepared baking pans and bake for 38 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
