## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Line pans with parchment rounds, and grease with non-stick cooking spray.
2. Mix together all dry ingredients (flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt) in a stand mixer with a paddle until fully combined.
3. Mix chunks of room-temperature butter slowly into the dry mix, on a low speed. Continue to mix until no large chunks of butter remain, and the mixture becomes crumbly.
4. Pour in egg whites and mix on low until just incorporated.
5. Mix in the buttermilk in two installments, on a low speed.
6. Add in vanilla extract, almond extract, and oil, and mix at a low speed until fully incorporated. Scrape down the sides of the bowl with a spatula, then beat on low speed for about 15 seconds (the key is to mix until the ingredients are JUST incorporated, so that the batter isn't over-mixed).
7. If desired, add in gel food coloring. Mix the color in by hand using a rubber spatula, scraping the sides and bottom of the bowl part way through. Mix until the batter is evenly colored.
8. Fold the sprinkles into the cake batter using a rubber spatula, just they've evenly distributed.
9. Divide batter evenly between the prepared cake pans.
10. Bake for 34-37 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out clean. Allow the layers to cool.
