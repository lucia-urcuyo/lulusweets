## Ingredients

| Ingredients | 9 in (pie) | 9 in (cheesecake) | 6 in (cheesecake) |
| :---- | :---: | :---: | :---: |
| Graham Crackers Crumbs | 180.9 | 164 | 82 |
| Sugar | 35.1 | 36 | 18 |
| Unsalted Butter | 100.8 | 77 | 38.5 |
| Salt | 2 | 2 | 1 |

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
2. Add the graham cracker crumbs, sugar, and salt to a large bowl and whisk to combine.
3. Add the melted butter and stir until well combined.
4. Pour the mixture into the pie pan or cheesecake springform pan and spread the crumbs around so that they are evenly dispersed.
5. Press the crumbs into the bottom and up the sides of the pan. Pack it firmly.
6. Bake for 13 minutes. Allow to cool.
